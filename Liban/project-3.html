AfyaCare: Patient Registration and Appointment Scheduling System

AfyaCare introduces a seamless Patient Registration and Appointment Scheduling system via a mobile application. This user-friendly platform empowers patients to efficiently schedule medical appointments, receive timely notifications including appointment details and checkup results. Additionally, AfyaCare enhances patient engagement by providing medication availability updates and pertinent information from the hospital's pharmacy. Emphasizing continuity of care, the application ensures patients are well-informed about their upcoming appointments, fostering a holistic and patient-centric healthcare experience.
