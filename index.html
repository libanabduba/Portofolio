Resure

Resure is car sale price prediction mobile application for individuals who want to predict a car value based on car brand, model, year of manufacture, fuel type, transmission type and others.

Case Study

Coffeenet

Coffeenet is a mobile app that captures images of coffee leaves, employing image recognition to identify and categorize diseases swiftly. It enables farmers to make timely interventions for maintaining the health and productivity of their coffee plantations.

Case Study

AfyaCare

AfyaCare is a mobile app offering a Patient Registration and Appointment Scheduling system. It allows patients to conveniently schedule medical appointments, receive notifications about scheduled checkups, access checkup results in their medical records, and stay informed about prescribed medications and pharmacy details for efficient healthcare management.

Case Study

Imala

Imala is a web application for efficient tariff management, allowing passengers to check tariffs and perform account-related actions. Registered users can report illegal tax raises, while authorized personnel can conveniently access and review record counts for enhanced transparency.
